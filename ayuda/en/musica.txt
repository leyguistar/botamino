join: put the bot in voice chat.
leave: makes the bot leave the voice chat.
live: start a live mode.
pause: pause what is playing.
play: play a song.
playing: shows what is playing.
projection: start a projection.
queue: shows the playback queue.
remove: remove a user from the voice participants' chat.
skip: skip the current song.
videocall: start a video call.
volume: change the volume.
