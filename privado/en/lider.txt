[c] Leader commands:
enable: disables a command in all the public chats of this community, however the commands will still works in private chats.
disable: enable a command in the community.
accept: accept [leader|curator] makes the bot a leader or curator.
config: shows the bot's current configuration in the community.
message: [wall|private] configures the bot's default welcome on the wall or when someone opens private chat.

[ci] Note: There are more exclusive commands for leaders but these are intended for group chats such as the staff chat
