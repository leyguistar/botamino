This community does not currently have any bot assigned, to take a bot the community has to meet certain conditions.

1. That the leaders allow the bots of ley: For this some leader of the community has to go to tell ley personally that he wants a bot and they are allowed in the community.

2. The community has to have rules for bots: So that they are not misused, the community has to have a blog or a wiki explaining what they are, what things are allowed and what not.

3. The bots have to have some kind of verification, either by means of a title or any creative way that the bots come up with to confirm that the bots are really accepted by the staff they have to have a brand.
